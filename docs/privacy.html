Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Vortex ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Vortex app.

1. Information We Collect

Anonymous Identifier. When you open Vortex, we create an anonymous account. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifying information.

Messages & Content. Messages you write and submit to Vortex are stored on our servers and expire automatically after 24–48 hours.

Usage Data. We collect data about how you interact with the app: messages guessed, guess results, streaks, and city preferences. This data is linked to your anonymous ID only.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:

  • Provide the core app experience (message feed, guessing game)
  • Calculate city mood scores and leaderboards
  • Send local push notifications (daily challenge alerts, streak reminders)
  • Improve app content quality

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Vortex uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • OpenAI — AI-generated message content
  • Reddit / Arctic Shift — public post aggregation for city content

4. Data Retention

Anonymous user profiles are retained until you delete your account. Messages expire automatically after 24–48 hours. Guesses and usage statistics are retained for leaderboard calculations.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time via Settings → Delete Account inside the app. Upon deletion, your profile, guesses, and identifiable data are permanently removed.

6. Children's Privacy

Vortex is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us.

7. Push Notifications

Vortex may send local push notifications for daily challenges and streak reminders. You can disable these at any time in your device Settings app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions? Contact us at: support@vortexapp.io
